Vision
Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Hyderabad targets to stimulate and encourage entrepreneurship and innovation both within IIT Hyderabad and beyond. After all, understanding the value of budding business ideas today and supporting the students to get market exposure and the right direction for their start-up is the only way to utilize their latent inventive potential
Global Network
We aim to facilitate start-ups of IITH with the right connection based on the nature of start-up, industry, mentoring requirements. This initiative will help start-ups to raise funds, find advisors, establish strategic partnerships with business leaders and take a step ahead towards creating a high-impact business.

OUR INITIATIVES

Kick Start

An opportunity to showcase the potential of your budding startups. The winning entries will be awarded monetary investment from E-Cell.

E-Summit

E-Summit is the annual flagship event of E-Cell IIT Hyderabad. It is aimed at making the campus the biggest networking hub in the country during the event. It brings together Startups, Entrepreneurs, Students, Investors, VCs and everyone involved in the startup ecosystem.

Young Entrepreneur Award

The Young Entrepreneur Award provides a platform for school students who are aspiring to become entrepreneurs to pitch their ideas and turn them into reality.
