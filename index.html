Vision
Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Hyderabad targets to stimulate and encourage entrepreneurship and innovation both within IIT Hyderabad and beyond. After all, understanding the value of budding business ideas today and supporting the students to get market exposure and the right direction for their start-up is the only way to utilize their latent inventive potential
Global Network
To bring global business leaders closer to the IIT Hyderabad community, E-Cell IITH has launched the Global network initiative. With this, we aim to facilitate start-ups of IITH with the right connection based on the nature of start-up, industry, mentoring requirements. This initiative will help start-ups to raise funds, find advisors, establish strategic partnerships with business leaders and take a step ahead towards creating a high-impact business.
Idea Validation
E-Cell IITH conducts the Idea Validation Camp to foster an entrepreneurship culture among students and showcase students' critical and creative thinking skills in finding a solution to a real-life problem through a start-up idea. The industry experts evaluate these ideas and offer valuable insights and suggestions which helps students to gain confidence and exposure at the initial stage of start-up/ entrepreneurial journey.
E-Networking
We E-Cell IIT Hyderabad have started a new initiative called E-Networking under which we are collaborating with institutes from all over India to build a long term strong PAN-India community of entrepreneurship and innovation promoting bodies. This program aims to serve as a platform to share and publicize any entrepreneurship-related events in a network of other like-minded people and also, to help the relatively newer organizations to grow by providing them guidance and contacts.
